Victim recovering after weekend truck-bicycle crash
LANSING — A bicyclist who was hit by a pickup truck Saturday morning on Lansing’s south side is recovering from her injuries, Lansing police said.
An investigation showed the driver of the pickup was not at fault in the 5:30 a.m. crash at the intersection of Cedar and Northrup streets, Lansing police Public Information Officer Robert Merritt said.
The bicyclist, a woman in her late 40s, was hospitalized with serious injuries but is expected to survive, Merritt said. Police have not released her name or the name of the driver.
